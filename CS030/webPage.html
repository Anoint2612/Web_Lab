Web Development Lab

A Web Development Lab at Ramaiah Institute of Technology provides a hands-on learning environment for designing, developing, and deploying websites and web applications. It covers essential technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enabling students to build structured, styled, and interactive web pages. Through practical exercises, learners gain experience in responsive design, DOM manipulation, and front-end frameworks. Additionally, backend technologies, databases, and deployment strategies may be explored to create full-stack applications. The lab encourages problem-solving, creativity, and collaboration, helping students understand real-world web development practices.
